!Breaking News! Indie Game Developer Breaks Into Fullstack Development

Hi there, I'm Jess. I'm a software tester, indie game developer, and amateur AI researcher. I made this page to satisfy the webpage creation requirement for the AZNext Full Stack Developer Certification.

Click here to see more info on the Full Stack Developer Certificate

So why did I choose to make this? Honestly, partially to amuse myself I as I worked. Hand coding is not my favorite thing to do but the assignment was to hand code a webpage to prove basic understanding, so that is what I was going to do. However, this design also serves as an excellent opportunity for me to showcase knowledge in a variety of places as well as reference other things I am working on. For example, I will also use it to mention my in development game, Chronicles of the Kethaneum.

I hope to get a few things out of this effort. My first hope is that I prove I am sufficiently well versed in web technologies. But beyond that, I want to take this full stack developer course to round out my programming undrestanding. I actually use AI help with most of my projects but AI help is never a substitute for the human knowing their stuff. So, in order to make better and better programs with my AIs, I want to improve my end of the project and make sure that I know what I'm asking for and how I am directing the work. Additionally, I hope that I can draw attention to my personal projects and make you smile.

So what is Chronicles of the Kethaneum, you may have asked? Or maybe you were just wondering when I will shut up... Well, I can't answer the second question but I can answer the first! Chronicles of the Kethaneum is a narrative driven word search game. I originally came up with the concept as a prototype for my game development bootcamp. After the bootcamp, I decided that I would actually see if it was viable ot try to build the game I envisioned. I was shocked by how much I was able to accomplish so fast while working with AIs
